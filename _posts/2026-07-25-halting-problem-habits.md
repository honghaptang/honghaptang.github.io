# Week 18: Philosophy

## Turing's Question

Can we determine if a program will halt? No. Undecidable.

## The Habit Question

Can we determine if a habit will stick? Also no. Also undecidable.

## The Proof (Sketch)

Assume we can predict habit success. Then:
1. I know "write weekly" will succeed
2. I relax, skip this week
3. The habit fails
4. Contradiction

Assume I know it will fail:
1. I know "write weekly" will fail
2. I try harder, succeed this week
3. The habit might stick
4. Contradiction

Therefore: undecidable.

## The Practical Implication

You can't know if you'll maintain a habit. You can only:
1. Start
2. Continue
3. Restart when you stop

## The Blog as Habit

18 weeks. 18 posts. The streak exists. The streak will break. The streak will restart.

This is the halting problem of the soul: you can't know if you'll persist. You can only persist and see.

## The Theorem

> **The Habit Halting Theorem:** The success of a habit is undecidable from the perspective of the habit-former.
>
> **Proof:** See above. See life. ∎
