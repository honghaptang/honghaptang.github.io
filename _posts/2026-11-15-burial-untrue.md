# Week 34: Music

## The Album

*Untrue* (2007) by Burial. Anonymous producer. South London. The most influential electronic album of the 2000s.

## The Sound

```
Tempo: 140 BPM (half-time feels like 70)
Elements: Crackle, vinyl noise, pitched vocal samples, sub-bass
Mood: Rainy night, empty streets, longing
```

## The Technique

Burial doesn't use clean samples. He uses:
- YouTube rips (low quality)
- Vinyl crackle (added noise)
- Pitched vocals (chopped, reversed)

The result: haunted music. Ghosts in the machine.

## The Connection to Saudade

*Untrue* is saudade in album form. The longing for something that never existed. The memories of places you've never been.

## The Tracks

1. **"Archangel"** — The hit. Vocal sample: "I love you so."
2. **"Near Dark"** — 140 BPM of pure atmosphere.
3. **"Untrue"** — The title track. The thesis.

## The Legacy

*Untrue* proved electronic music could be emotional. Could be sad. Could be art.

Burial disappeared. The music remains.
