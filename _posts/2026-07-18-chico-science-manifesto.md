# Week 17: Music

## The Caranguejo

Chico Science didn't invent manguebeat. He named it. Gave it teeth.

> *"Caranguejo não é peixe, caranguejo é caranguejo."*

A crab isn't a fish. A crab is a crab. Manguebeat isn't rock, isn't hip-hop, isn't maracatu. It's manguebeat.

## The Sound

Take:
- Maracatu (Pernambuco folk tradition)
- Funk (James Brown, 1970s)
- Hip-hop (Public Enemy, 1980s)
- Metal (Sepultura, 1990s)
- Electronic (Aphex Twin, 1990s)

Mix. Serve cold. Recife, 1994.

## The Tracks

1. **"Da Lama ao Caos"** — From mud to chaos. The title track.
2. **"Manguetown"** — The manifesto. "Manguetown, que tédio!"
3. **"A Cidade"** — The city. Recife as character.

## The Math

BPM of maracatu: ~120
BPM of funk: ~100
BPM of manguebeat: ~140

The average isn't the point. The tension is.

## The Legacy

Chico died in 1997. Car accident. 30 years old. Manguebeat lived.

Nação Zumbi continued. Others followed. The crab walked on land and sea.
