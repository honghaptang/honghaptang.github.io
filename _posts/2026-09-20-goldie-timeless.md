# Week 26: Music

## The Album

*Timeless* (1995) by Goldie. Two hours. One CD. The longest drum and bass album ever made.

## The Title Track

"Timeless" — 21 minutes. Not a song — a journey.

```
00:00 — Intro, strings
03:00 — Breakbeat enters
07:00 — Bass drops
12:00 — Vocal sample: "I'm timeless"
15:00 — Breakdown, strings return
18:00 — Final drop
21:00 — Fade
```

## The Mathematics

At 172 BPM:
- 21 minutes = 21 × 172 × 4 / 60 ≈ 241 bars
- Each bar = 60/172 × 4 ≈ 1.4 seconds
- 241 bars × 1.4 seconds = 337 seconds = 5.6 minutes... wait, that's wrong

The math doesn't matter. The feeling does.

## The Legacy

*Timeless* proved drum and bass could be album-length. Could be art. Could be more than club fodder.

## The Connection

Chico Science's *Da Lama ao Caos* — 40 minutes.
Roni Size's *New Forms* — 75 minutes.
Goldie's *Timeless* — 127 minutes.

The pattern: expand the form. Break the constraints. Make it timeless.
