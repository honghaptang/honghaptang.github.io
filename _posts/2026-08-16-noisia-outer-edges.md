# Week 21: Music

## The Album

*Outer Edges* (2017) by Noisia. Not an album — an expedition. Four Dutch producers mapping the boundary of drum and bass.

## The Territory

| Track | BPM | Key Feature |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| "Machine Gun" | 174 | Literally sounds like gunfire |
| "Voodoo" | 172 | Bass that shouldn't exist |
| "Tentacles" | 175 | The title says it all |
| "Diplodopus" | 174 | Jurassic bass |

## The Production

Noisia doesn't use samples. They synthesize everything. The bass in "Voodoo" isn't a recording — it's math.

```
Oscillator 1: Sine wave, 50 Hz
Oscillator 2: Sawtooth, 100 Hz
Filter: Lowpass, resonance at 2 kHz
LFO: Modulating cutoff at 1/16 notes
```

## The Philosophy

The "outer edges" aren't a place — they're a direction. You don't arrive. You travel.
