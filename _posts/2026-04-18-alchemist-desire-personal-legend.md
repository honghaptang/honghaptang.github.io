# Week 4: Books & Philosophy

## The Alchemist Revisited

Paulo Coelho's *The Alchemist* gets dismissed as self-help fiction. That's a mistake.

The book's core thesis: **the universe conspires to help you achieve your Personal Legend**. Not your goal. Not your dream. Your *Legend* — the thing you're meant to do before you die.

Santiago learns:
1. The map is not the territory (the treasure isn't where the dream says)
2. The journey transforms the traveler (the boy becomes the alchemist)
3. Love doesn't anchor — it liberates (Fatima waits, but doesn't chain)

## Turing's Boiling Water

*"The boiling water in Turing's pot"* — what a title. 

Turing asked: can machines think? The water boils, but does it know it's boiling? The algorithm solves P vs NP, but does it understand the proof?

When 이세돌 played move 78, he did something no AI could: he played *beautifully*. The move had style. AlphaGo won, but it didn't dance.
