# Week 20: Multilingual

## The Station

Radio Julio. Not a real station. Or maybe it is. I wrote about it in June 2025. The name stuck.

## The Frequency

Shortwave radio: 3-30 MHz. Signals bounce off the ionosphere. Travel thousands of kilometers. A station in Brazil can be heard in Mongolia.

## The Languages

Shortwave is polyglot by nature:
- BBC World Service (English)
- Radio China (Mandarin, 40+ languages)
- Radio Romania (Romanian, 12 languages)
- Vatican Radio (Latin, yes, Latin)

## The Portuguese Connection

```
Rádio Internacional de Portugal
Frequência: 9765 kHz
Idioma: Português
```

The signal reaches Angola. Mozambique. Brazil. The former empire, reunited by radio waves.

## The Korean Connection

```
KBS World Radio
Frequency: 11730 kHz
Language: Korean, 11 languages
```

North Korean defectors listen. The signal crosses the DMZ. Words as contraband.

## The Blog as Radio

This blog broadcasts on no frequency. Reaches no ionosphere. But the impulse is the same: speak, and hope someone listens.
