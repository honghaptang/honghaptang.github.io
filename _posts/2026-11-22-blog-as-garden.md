# Week 35: Philosophy

## The Metaphor

A blog is not a publication. It's a garden.

- Posts are plants
- Some thrive
- Some die
- Some grow in unexpected directions

## The Gardening Practices

| Practice | Blog Equivalent |
|----------|-----------------|
| Planting | Writing new posts |
| Watering | Revisiting, updating |
| Pruning | Deleting, editing |
| Weeding | Removing outdated content |

## My Garden

35 posts. Some I'm proud of. Some I'd prune if I could. All part of the ecosystem.

## The Comparison

**Publication model:** Polished, final, perfect.
**Garden model:** Growing, changing, alive.

This blog is a garden. The weekly cadence is the watering schedule.

## The Seasons

- Spring: New ideas, fresh posts
- Summer: Growth, expansion
- Autumn: Reflection, harvest
- Winter: Dormancy, rest

We're in late autumn. 35 weeks in. The leaves are turning.

## The Promise

A garden doesn't promise perfection. It promises life.

This blog promises: I'll show up. I'll write. I'll tend the garden.

The rest is weather.
