# Week 16: Multilingual

## The Date

March 2016. 이세돌 vs. AlphaGo. Game 4. Move 78.

## The Korean

**신의 한 수** — *sin-ui han su* — the divine move.

Not "great move." Not "clever move." *Divine*. From the gods.

## The Context

AlphaGo was winning. 3-0 in the series. Unbeatable. Then move 78:

```
   A B C D E F G H J K L M N O P Q R S T
19 . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
...
```

The commentators gasped. Lee Sedol placed the stone. The board changed.

## What the Machine Couldn't Do

AlphaGo calculated probabilities. Lee Sedol played *beauty*. The move wasn't optimal — it was necessary. It said: "I am human. I create."

## The Aftermath

Lee Sedol won game 4. Lost the series 4-1. Retired in 2019, saying AI could never be defeated.

But move 78 remains. A testament. A scar. A divine mistake that wasn't.

## The Translation

| Korean | English | Meaning |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 신의 한 수 | Divine move | God's single play |
| 수가 빛났다 | The move shone | Move was brilliant |
| 인간らしい | Human-like | Like a person |

The machine played perfectly. The human played divinely.
