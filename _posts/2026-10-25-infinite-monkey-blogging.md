# Week 31: Philosophy

## The Theorem

A monkey typing randomly for infinite time will eventually produce Shakespeare.

## The Blog Corollary

A blogger writing weekly for infinite time will eventually produce... something meaningful?

## The Probability

Let's say:
- Each post is 500 words
- Each word has 10,000 possible choices
- Probability of a "good" post = 1/100

After 52 posts (one year):
- Probability of at least one great post = 1 - (99/100)^52 ≈ 41%

After 260 posts (five years):
- Probability ≈ 93%

## The Reality

I'm not typing randomly. I'm typing with intent. The probability is higher. Or lower. Depends on the day.

## The Monkey's Advantage

The monkey doesn't judge. The monkey types. The monkey produces Shakespeare eventually.

I judge. I edit. I delete. I produce... 31 posts in 52 weeks.

## The Lesson

Be more monkey. Type more. Judge less. Shakespeare will come. Or not. The typing is the point.
