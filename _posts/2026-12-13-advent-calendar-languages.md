# Week 38: Multilingual

## The Concept

An advent calendar has 24 doors. One for each day leading to Christmas. Behind each door: a chocolate. Or a language.

## The Pattern

Romance languages share roots. Portuguese, Spanish, French, Italian, Romanian — all Latin's children.

Germanic languages share structure. German, English, Dutch — siblings.

Korean stands alone. Language isolate. Beautiful, lonely.

Lojban stands apart. Constructed. Logical. Alien.

## The Gift

The gift isn't the greeting. It's the understanding. Each language is a door. Behind it: a world.

## The 25th Door

January 1. A new year. A new language? Or the same languages, deeper?
